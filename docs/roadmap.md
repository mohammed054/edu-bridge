# Hikmah School - AI Demo Script

## Demo Principle
- Focus on AI-generated outputs only.
- Show insights, not menus or navigation.
- Keep the experience calm, premium, and structured.
- Use color-coded summaries for fast comprehension.
- UI language is Arabic with English numbers.

## Master Demo Data (Use Across All Screens)
- اسم الطالب: أحمد خالد
- الصف: Grade 8
- مستوى الخطورة (AI): Medium
- إكمال الواجبات: 60%
- الاستبيان الأسبوعي: 0 / 1
- الحضور: 95%
- مخالفات السلوك: 2 (minor repeated)
- اتجاه التحصيل: Declining (Math down 5%)

## Screen 1 - Welcome / Student Snapshot
### Purpose
Introduce an AI risk snapshot for one student.

### What Client Sees
- Student photo card with `اسم الطالب`.
- Risk badge: `منخفض` / `متوسط` / `مرتفع` (AI-calculated).
- Auto-generated summary table:

| المؤشر | الحالة | ملاحظة AI |
|---|---|---|
| إكمال الواجبات | 60% | يوجد 2 واجبات ناقصة |
| الاستبيانات | 0 / 1 | لم يتم تسليم الاستبيان الأسبوعي |
| الحضور | 95% | مستقر |
| مخالفات السلوك | 2 | ملاحظات بسيطة متكررة |
| الاتجاه الأكاديمي | Declining | مادة الرياضيات انخفضت 5% |

### Talking Points
- "The AI calculated this student risk automatically."
- "It combines homework, behavior, attendance, and engagement."
- "Teachers get a clear snapshot without manual guesswork."

## Screen 2 - Homework Insights
### Purpose
Show AI analysis of homework performance.

### What Client Sees
- Auto-populated assignment table:

| الواجب | تاريخ الاستحقاق | الحالة | ملاحظة AI |
|---|---|---|---|
| Math HW1 | 22/02/2026 | Done | أداء جيد |
| Science HW2 | 23/02/2026 | Missing | يحتاج متابعة |
| Weekly Survey | 20/02/2026 | Late | يفضل تعزيز الالتزام |

- Highlight chips:
  - `Completion Rate: 60%`
  - `Missing: 1`
  - `Late: 1`

### Talking Points
- "AI identifies homework patterns automatically."
- "Missing and late work are flagged instantly."
- "No manual tracking is required."

## Screen 3 - Behavior & Attendance Insights
### Purpose
Show AI pattern detection from behavior and attendance logs.

### What Client Sees
- Summary cards:
  - `الحضور: 95%` [Green]
  - `مخالفات السلوك: 2` [Yellow]
  - `تفاعل ولي الأمر: Medium` [Blue]
- Optional compact chart: weekly behavior trend with green/yellow/red markers.

### Talking Points
- "AI combines attendance and behavior logs in one view."
- "It detects repeated low-level issues early."
- "Intervention suggestions appear automatically."

## Screen 4 - Risk Index Overview
### Purpose
Explain how AI computes student risk.

### What Client Sees
- Risk badge: `مستوى الخطورة: متوسط`.
- Horizontal risk bar:
  - Green = Low
  - Yellow = Medium
  - Red = High
- Key contributors list:
  - `الواجبات: 2 ناقص`
  - `السلوك: 2 مخالفات بسيطة`
  - `الحضور: مستقر`
  - `التفاعل: متوسط`

### Talking Points
- "Risk is computed automatically from multiple sources."
- "Teachers can instantly see who needs extra support."
- "The model is transparent through visible contributors."

## Screen 5 - Feedback Analysis
### Purpose
Show AI rewriting teacher feedback into a parent-friendly format.

### What Client Sees
- Original teacher note:
  - "Ahmed did not submit his survey and missed homework."
- AI rewritten message (parent-facing):
  - "أحمد أنجز معظم الواجبات لكنه لم يسلّم استبيان واحد. المتابعة الأسبوعية ستساعده على تحسين الأداء."
- Approval controls:
  - `اعتماد`
  - `تعديل`
  - `إرسال`

### Talking Points
- "AI keeps communication clear and professional."
- "Tone is softened while preserving the facts."
- "Teacher approval remains in control before sending."

## Screen 6 - OCR / Auto Data Capture
### Purpose
Show AI automating grade entry from teacher records.

### What Client Sees
- Upload panel for screenshot of `سجل المتابعة`.
- Extracted results table:

| المادة | HW | Exam | Final | السلوك | ملاحظة AI |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Math | 3 / 5 | 70 | 80 | Stable | Trend stable |
| Science | 2 / 5 | 65 | 75 | Needs attention | Needs survey completion |

### Talking Points
- "AI reads data directly from screenshots."
- "Academic records update instantly without manual entry."
- "This saves significant teacher time."

## Screen 7 - AI Recommendations & Nudges
### Purpose
Show actionable AI-generated recommendations.

### What Client Sees
- Teacher recommendations:
  - `إرسال تذكير بشأن الاستبيان غير المكتمل`
  - `تعزيز إيجابي قبل واجب الرياضيات القادم`
- Parent recommendations:
  - `متابعة الواجبات أسبوعياً`
  - `حجز متابعة في حال بقاء الخطورة Medium/High`
- Priority labels: `High`, `Medium`, `Low`.

### Talking Points
- "AI does not only report data, it recommends actions."
- "Teachers and parents know the next step immediately."

## Screen 8 - Weekly Schedule AI Overview
### Purpose
Show unified weekly view with AI status classification.

### What Client Sees
- Weekly calendar containing classes, homework deadlines, and exams.
- Status colors:
  - Green = Completed
  - Yellow = Pending
  - Red = Missed
- AI callout: `الأسبوع القادم يحتاج تركيز على Math + Survey`.

### Talking Points
- "AI consolidates academic and admin signals in one glance."
- "Students and teachers can prioritize the week quickly."

## Screen 9 - Advanced AI Insights (Optional)
### Purpose
Show class-level intelligence, not only student-level insight.

### What Client Sees
- Aggregate class insights:
  - `5 طلاب لم يسلّموا الاستبيان الأسبوع الماضي`
  - `متوسط Math انخفض 4% خلال 2 أسبوع`
- Auto-generated class-wide parent message suggestion.
- Suggested intervention block for the homeroom teacher.

### Talking Points
- "AI scales from individual to class-level analysis."
- "It reduces workload while improving parent communication quality."

## Demo Flow (Presenter Order)
1. Snapshot
2. Homework
3. Behavior + Attendance
4. Risk Index
5. Feedback Rewrite
6. OCR Capture
7. Recommendations
8. Weekly Overview
9. Optional Class Insights

## Demo Delivery Tips
- Keep focus on AI outputs at every step.
- Use realistic student context consistently across screens.
- Show only key tables/charts; avoid dense raw grids.
- Emphasize time saved, clarity, and decision support.
